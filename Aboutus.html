We Are Cape Threads

A fashion movement established in Cape Town in 2025, rooted in the city's bold streetwear culture.

Our Story
<>p> Cape Threads was founded in 2025 by a young, enthusiastic entrepreneur who observed that many people wanted modern fashion but didn't always have access to it locally. The idea started as a small online store selling a curated selection of T-shirts and sneakers through social media. As demand grew, the business gained popularity among students and young adults for its stylish range and reasonable prices.

Within less than a year, Cape Threads had saved enough to open its first physical store in a prominent Cape Town location. The store quickly earned a reputation for its modern sneakers, welcoming staff, and frequent promotions. By concentrating on customer feedback and trend forecasting, the range expanded to include apparel, accessories, and seasonal goods. Today, Cape Threads is expanding with intentions to establish additional locations and grow its online presence.

Mission

To make premium streetwear and footwear accessible to Cape Town's bold, fashion-forward community — one drop at a time.

Vision

To become the leading streetwear brand in South Africa — known for style, affordability, and a strong local identity across multiple stores and online.
